Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti noodles)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste (optional)
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste (if using), oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, letting the flavors meld.
  5. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the pasta water.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce in the pan. Add a splash or two of reserved pasta water if needed to create a desired consistency.
  7. Serve topped with fresh basil (if using) and grated Parmesan cheese.
